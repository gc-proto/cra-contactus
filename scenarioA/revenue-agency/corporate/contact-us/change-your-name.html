If you are contacting us on someone else's behalf

If you wish to deal with the CRA on someone else’s behalf, you must be an authorized representative:

  • authorizing or cancelling a representative (personal)
By mail or fax:
Mail or fax us to change your name

Send a letter with the following:

  • the original or certified true copy of one of the following documents:
    • a name change certificate from a provincial/territorial vital statistics department; or
    • a court order issued under an act on change of name;
  • your old and new names;
  • your social insurance number; and
  • your signature.

The letter should be sent by mail or fax to your tax centre:

  • Find your tax centre
